Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

The short version: BetterTuner does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All audio is processed on your device in real time and is never recorded or saved.

Microphone access

BetterTuner requests the microphone permission (RECORD_AUDIO) for one purpose: detecting the pitch of your instrument or voice so the tuner, visualizers, and training exercises can work.

  • Audio is analyzed on-device, in memory, in real time.
  • No audio is ever recorded, saved to storage, or transmitted anywhere.
  • Audio processing stops when you leave the app or revoke the permission.

Data collection

BetterTuner collects no data. The app has no internet permission, which means it is technically incapable of sending anything off your device. There are:

  • No analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising or ad identifiers
  • No accounts, sign-ins, or profiles
  • No crash reporting services
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Local settings

Your preferences (selected instrument and tuning, reference pitch, metronome patterns, training statistics, and similar settings) are stored only on your device in the app's private storage. They are not backed up to the cloud by the app (allowBackup is disabled) and are deleted when you uninstall the app.

Children

BetterTuner is suitable for all ages. Because the app collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If a future version of BetterTuner ever changes how it handles data, this page will be updated before that version is released, and the effective date above will change.
